Welcome to the Help Page!

We understand that sometimes you may have questions or need assistance while navigating our website. This Help Page is designed to provide you with the information you need to make your experience smooth and enjoyable. Below, you'll find some frequently asked questions and helpful resources to address common inquiries. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for further assistance.

    Account and Login:

  • How do I create an accountEnter your email and password given to you by our company
  • What should I do if I can't log in?If you cannot log in ensure that your membership has not expired and that you are entering correct information

    Purchase and Delivery

  • What payment methods are accepted?You need to create an account with us which you would deposit money in for you to make purchases
  • Can I track my order status?You will be emailed updates on your purchase
  • How long does shipping usually take?Shipping is done every Friday
  • What are the shipping fees?Shipping fee is always 10% of your purchase

    Returns and Refunds:

  • What is your return policy?You cannot return any tickets 10 days before the event
  • How do I initiate a return or exchange?You can email us to request a return or exchange but not 10 days before the event
  • When can I expect a refund?You can expect a return charge of 5% of your ticket price

    Technical Support:

  • I'm experiencing technical difficulties. What should I do?Contact us via email

For any other inquiries or issues not covered above, please contact our support team directly through the provided channels. We are here to assist you and ensure you have a seamless experience on our website.

Thank you for choosing our services. We appreciate your trust and look forward to serving you!
