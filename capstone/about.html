HISTORY


Sports Play is the newest sports ticket app in Grenada that provides an online platform for grenadians to buy their tickets for their sporting events. It was launched on May the 20th, 2023 by the sports play company to make purchasing tickets easier and quicker. Our online method of purchasing ticket for Football, Athletics and Cricket has been highly requested as we also deliver our items to customers and also give back to them for trusting us to deliver great service.
